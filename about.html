About Us

Shree Senthur Tiles was established in the year 2014 by its visionary founder, Mr. Madhanagopal, the son of Mr. Nallasamy. Initially, our business focused solely on tiles, but as we grew and expanded, we diversified into pipes and bathroom appliances to meet the evolving needs of our customers. Over the past decade, we have built a strong reputation and have successfully served more than 700 satisfied customers.

Our product range includes an extensive variety of tiles, such as bathroom tiles, elevation tiles, flooring tiles, and pooja tiles, each carefully selected to enhance the aesthetic appeal and functionality of your spaces. At Shree Senthur Tiles, we are committed to quality and excellence, dealing exclusively in premium brands like KAG and Tecton to ensure 100% customer satisfaction with durable and superior products. Our unwavering focus on quality and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted name in the industry, and we continue to uphold these values in everything we do.
